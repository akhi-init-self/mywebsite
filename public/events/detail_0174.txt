The Ilan Lev Method

Movement Exploration: The Ilan Lev Method

 © Geoffrey Delahaye
 
 

 

In the workshop, we explore a wide range of movements from various angles and perspectives. It is designed to improve inner body awareness and develop our functional abilities. We explore movements while lying, sitting, or standing, using extensive mental imagery. The skill and understanding grow side by side, while limitations and pain disappear. Vitality and joy grow immensely.

 

We discover and connect with a source of movement that already exists within us and around us, allowing it to move and inspire us — without forcing it. When new channels of communication between different parts of the body emerge, new possibilities arise. These discoveries unfold into a celebration of movement and life.
